Admission Process & Eligibility

  1. 1.
    Research & Choose: Select the country and university that best suits your needs.
  2. 2.
    Check Eligibility: Ensure you meet the academic and language requirements for admission.
  3. 3.
    Prepare Documents: Gather necessary documents like transcripts, passport, and medical certificates.
  4. 4.
    Submit Application: Apply online or through a consultant with the required paperwork.
  5. 5.
    Receive Admission Letter: Once accepted, you will receive an official admission letter.
  6. 6.
    Apply for Visa: Submit your student visa application with all necessary documents.
  7. 7.
    Plan Your Travel: Book flights and arrange accommodation before departure.
  8. 8.
    Begin Your Journey: Attend orientation and start your MBBS program abroad!
